Just What it Sounds Like

Bow Labs is a small and focussed company experimenting with a variety of software products. Recent applications are targeted specifically at crowd sourcing and micro labor. Bow Labs is based in northern California.


Bow Labs was incorporated in 2012 by Ozzie Gooen and Rahul Swaminathan. Ozzie and Rahul are recent graduates from Harvey Mudd College, where they learned web development and created a several profitable web companies. Some of these have won hackathons and/or been featured in media such as LifeHacker and the Wall Street Journal.
